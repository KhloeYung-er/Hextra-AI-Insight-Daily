### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We totally use Cookies and other similar tracking technologies to keep tabs on activity on our Service and stash some info. These tracking tech tools — we're talking beacons, tags, and scripts — help us collect and track info, plus improve and analyze our Service. The tech we might use includes:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a small file chillin' on Your Device. You can totally tell Your browser to refuse all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But hey, if you don't accept Cookies, you might not be able to use certain parts of our Service. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to reject Cookies, our Service might just use 'em.
- **Web Beacons**: Some sections of our Service and our emails might contain tiny electronic files called Web Beacons (also known as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These little guys let the Company count users who've visited those pages or opened an email, and for other related website stats (like checking out the popularity of a certain section and verifying system and server integrity). They're basically tiny trackers doing their thing! 👀

Cookies can be either "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. What's the diff? Persistent Cookies stick around on your personal computer or mobile device even when you're offline, while Session Cookies vanish the moment you close your web browser. Poof! 💨

We totally use both Session and Persistent Cookies for these purposes:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are absolutely crucial to give you the services available through the Website and to let you use some of its cool features. They help authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for can't be provided, and we only use these Cookies to give you those services. They're the backbone, basically! 💪

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are used to check if users have accepted the use of cookies on the Website. It's how we know you're cool with our cookie game! 😉

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices you make when you use the Website, like remembering your login deets or language preference. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience and save you from having to re-enter your preferences every single time you visit the Website. Super convenient, right? ✨

For even more info on the Cookies we use and your options concerning them, just swing by our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy. We've got all the deets there!
