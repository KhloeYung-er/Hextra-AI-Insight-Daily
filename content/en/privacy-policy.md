### Tracking Tech & Cookies – The Lowdown 🍪

We totally use Cookies and other neat tracking tech to keep an eye on activity on our Service and store some info. This tracking tech includes cool stuff like beacons, tags, and scripts. We use 'em to collect and track info, helping us make our Service even better and analyze how it's being used. The tech we might use includes:

- **Cookies (or Browser Cookies)**: A Cookie is just a tiny file that gets placed on your Device. You can tell your browser to totally refuse all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But heads up! If you don't accept Cookies, you might miss out on some parts of our Service. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to reject 'em, our Service just might use Cookies.
- **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and our emails might have these small electronic files called Web Beacons (also known as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These little guys let the Company (for example) count users who've visited those pages or opened an email. They're also used for other related website stats, like tracking how popular a section is and making sure our systems and servers are running smoothly.

Cookies can be 'Persistent' or 'Session' Cookies. When you go offline, Persistent Cookies stay chilling on your personal computer or mobile device. Session Cookies? Poof! They vanish the second you close your web browser.

We're rocking both Session and Persistent Cookies for these awesome reasons:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Managed by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are super vital to give you the services available through the Website and to let you use its cool features. They help us verify users and prevent any sneaky, fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for simply can't be provided. So, we only use these Cookies to hook you up with those services.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Managed by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are all about figuring out if users have given a nod 👍 to the use of cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Managed by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are key because they let us remember choices you make when you're using the Website, like keeping track of your login deets or language preferences. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience and save you from having to re-enter your preferences every single time you hit up the Website.

Wanna know more about the Cookies we use and what your options are? Swing by our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of this Privacy Policy. We've got you covered!
